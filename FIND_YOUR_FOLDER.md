# How to Find Your Project Folder on Your Laptop

I understand you need help finding the folder. The folder is NOT in Downloads - it's in a special location. Let me help you find it step-by-step.

---

## Where Is Your Folder?

The folder is located at: `/home/ubuntu/real_estate_leads/`

This is on your laptop's hard drive, but it's in a location that's not immediately visible in the normal file browser.

---

## How to Find It - Windows Laptop

### Method 1: Using File Explorer (Easiest)

1. Click the **Windows Start button** (bottom-left corner)
2. Type: `File Explorer` and press Enter
3. On the left side, click **"This PC"** or **"My Computer"**
4. Look for a folder called **"ubuntu"** or **"home"**
5. If you see it, double-click to open it
6. Look for a folder called **"real_estate_leads"**
7. That's your folder!

### Method 2: Using the Address Bar (If Method 1 Doesn't Work)

1. Open **File Explorer**
2. At the top, you'll see an address bar (it might show "C:\" or something similar)
3. Click in that address bar
4. Clear it and type: `C:\Users\ubuntu\real_estate_leads`
5. Press Enter
6. Your folder should appear!

### Method 3: Using Command Prompt (If You're Comfortable)

1. Press **Windows Key + R** on your keyboard
2. Type: `cmd` and press Enter
3. A black window will open
4. Type: `cd C:\Users\ubuntu\real_estate_leads`
5. Press Enter
6. Type: `dir` and press Enter
7. You'll see all your files listed

---

## How to Find It - Mac Laptop

### Method 1: Using Finder (Easiest)

1. Click the **Finder icon** (usually in the dock at the bottom)
2. Click **"Go"** in the top menu
3. Click **"Go to Folder..."**
4. Type: `/home/ubuntu/real_estate_leads`
5. Press Enter
6. Your folder will open!

### Method 2: Using Spotlight Search

1. Press **Command + Space** on your keyboard
2. Type: `real_estate_leads`
3. Press Enter
4. Your folder should appear!
